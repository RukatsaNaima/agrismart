🔍 From Assumptions to Insights: What I Learned from the Field

Key learnings that transformed our understanding of rural farming challenges

❌ MYTH

Farmers are resistant to new technology

✅ FACT

Farmers aren't resistant—they're underserved and eager for accessible solutions

❌ MYTH

Digital tools work the same everywhere

✅ FACT

Tools must fit rural realities: low literacy, limited bandwidth, basic devices

❌ MYTH

Text-based information is sufficient

✅ FACT

Visuals and voice features dramatically boost understanding and adoption

❌ MYTH

Expert knowledge is always trusted

✅ FACT

Community-driven knowledge is more trusted than top-down training
